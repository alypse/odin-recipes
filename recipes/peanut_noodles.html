Peanut Noodles

10 minutes for something this delicious?

These 10 minute peanut noodles are creamy, chewy, and delicious. This recipe only requires common Asian pantry ingredients that are not hard to find.

My favorite noodles to use are fresh jajang noodles which I will go into detail about later. And for those who don’t like it spicy, just leave out the chili crisp.

Ingredients:

  • 200g fresh jajang noodles
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tbsp rice vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp chili crisp
  • 2 tbsp hot water
  • ½ tsp sesame oil

Directions:

  1. Start by cooking your favorite noodles according to packaging then drain and make sure to save some of that noodle water for later.
  2. Add remaining ingredients into a bowl, then add 2 tbsp of the hot noodle water to melt everything together.
  3. Mix your noodles in, top with the garnishes and enjoy!
