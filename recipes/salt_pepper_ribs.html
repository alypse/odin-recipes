Chinese-style salt and pepper ribs

These Chinese salt & pepper ribs combine savory, sweet, and spicy flavors together that are sure to become a family favorite.

No slow cooking in the oven for 2 hours, all it takes is 30 minutes from start to finish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 rack spare ribs (10-12 ribs)
  • 2 tsp pepper salt
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp custard powder
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 green onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 red thai chilli

Directions:

  1. Cut up your rack of ribs into individual ribs and let them come up to room temperature if you have time.
  2. Season with pepper salt, soy sauce, sugar, custard powder, and oil.
  3. Air fry at 375F for 15 minutes flipping halfway through the cooking process. Make sure you don't overcrowd the air fryer.
  4. Chop up your garlic, green onions, and chili's and saut√© in a pan for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. Add the ribs from the air fryer into the pan and toss for an additional 3 minutes.
  6. Top with more green onions and enjoy!
